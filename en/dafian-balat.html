Clean Tile Backfill (Dafan) - A Strong and Level Base for Tiling and Backfilling

**Tile backfill (Dafan)** is one of the essential materials in construction and finishing projects, especially when preparing surfaces for tile or ceramic installation. Desert Sand Est. provides it in high quality, as it is clean and free of impurities and organic materials, which guarantees a solid and level base.

Tile backfill is used to fill gaps and level surfaces, making it ideal for backfilling and spreading work before pouring concrete or installing tiles. Its quality ensures no future settlement occurs, which maintains the stability and durability of floors in the long run.

Advantages of Tile Backfill from Desert Sand Est.

  • Clean and free of impurities and foreign materials to ensure high quality.
  • Ideal for leveling and backfilling work under tiles and ceramics.
  • Provides a solid and level base, preventing future settlement and cracks.
  • Helps absorb excess moisture and protect foundations.
  • Available in large quantities to meet the requirements of residential and commercial projects.
  • Fast and reliable delivery to work sites in Makkah.
  • Competitive prices that guarantee value for quality.

Common Uses of Tile Backfill

  • **Floor Leveling:** To create a uniform level before installing tiles or marble.
  • **Foundation Backfilling:** To fill gaps around foundations and increase their stability.
  • **Infrastructure Works:** In road and pavement projects as a base layer.
  • **Roof Gardens:** As an excellent drainage layer in elevated gardens.
  • **Preparing Walkways:** To prepare the ground before installing interlocking blocks or stone.
