FAQ: Your Comprehensive Guide to Building Materials and Our Services in Makkah

Here you will find clear and concise answers to your most common questions about the building materials we provide, including various types of sand, bat7a (Riyadh bat7a and Taima bat7a), dafan, tartasha, concrete, and **speez**. We strive to provide all the information you need to ensure the success of your projects.

Do you provide product installation services?

Yes, we provide installation services for all products that require it, to ensure quality and optimal performance. Please contact us for details.

You can easily order **building materials** by **calling us**, sending a **WhatsApp message**, or visiting the "Contact Us" page. Our team is happy to assist you.

Certainly! We are committed to providing **high-quality products** and professional services. All our **building materials** and services come with a warranty for your peace of mind.

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order. Please **contact customer service** immediately after confirming the order for assistance.

What is the difference between Riyadh and Taima bat7a?

**Riyadh bat7a** (fine and light-colored) is ideal for **interior plastering**. As for **Taima bat7a** (coarse and red), it is most suitable for **exterior facades and decorations**. For more, please visit the **Difference Between Riyadh and Taima bat7a page**.

What other types of sand do you have?

In addition to bat7a, we provide **white sand for construction**, **clean dafan sand**, and **washed sand**, and more to meet the needs of **construction projects**. Explore our **Products** and **Services** sections.

Uses of dafan, tartasha, and volcanic insulation?

**Dafan** is used for **leveling floors**. **Tartasha** is for **wall strength**. And **volcanic insulation** is for **thermal and water insulation of surfaces** and **electrical insulation**. More details in our **blog**.

Do you provide ready-mix concrete and speez (backfill)?

Yes, we provide **high-quality ready-mix concrete** for **slabs**, and **speez** (backfill materials) for **foundation stabilization** and **road backfilling** and construction.

Can I visit your location in Makkah?

Certainly! We are happy to have you visit us at our headquarters in **Makkah**. You can find our exact location on the map on the **Contact Us page**.

What are the prices for sand and bat7a?

The **prices for sand**, **bat7a**, and all **building materials** vary depending on the type and quantity. To get the **best price** and a customized quote, please **contact us** directly.
