The Key Difference Between Riyadh and Taima Bat7a for Construction Projects

Riyadh Bat7a: High Quality for Interior Plastering

Riyadh bat7a is considered one of the finest types of bat7a in the Kingdom. It is characterized by its light color and fine grains, making it the ideal choice for interior plastering and fine finishing. Its fineness helps achieve perfectly smooth and level surfaces, which reduces the effort required for final finishes and ensures a high aesthetic appeal. Its flexibility during application also reduces the chances of cracks appearing in the plaster after drying, and it is an essential material for achieving a professional finish on interior building walls.

Taima Bat7a: For Strength and Distinctive Exterior Decorations

In contrast, Taima bat7a is distinguished by its earthy red color and coarser, harder grains. These characteristics give it exceptional durability and high resistance to weather factors, making it ideal for use on exterior building facades and finishing works that require extreme durability. Taima bat7a is widely used for creating complex decorative and artistic patterns on facades and provides excellent cohesion with white cement to highlight colors and designs. Although the price of Taima bat7a may be slightly higher than Riyadh bat7a, its quality and long-term resistance to erosion make it a wise investment for exterior projects that demand distinction and durability.
