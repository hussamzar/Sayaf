Speez - An Essential Material for Foundation and Backfill Durability and Stability

Speez is a vital construction material known for its durability and high capacity to provide stability and support for foundations and backfilling work. Desert Sand Est. provides it in excellent quality, making it the ideal choice for projects that require superior load-bearing capacity and a long-lasting structure.

Speez is used in sub-bases, backfills, and under road and building floors to prevent future subsidence or deformation. Its cohesive and solid composition makes it resistant to weather and erosion, ensuring the continued strength of the foundations the project relies on.

Advantages of Speez from Desert Sand Est.

  • Superior durability and rigidity provide strong support for foundations.
  • Prevents subsidence and cracks in floors and foundations.
  • Resistant to weather and erosion, ensuring a long project life.
  • Suitable for a wide range of backfilling and leveling applications.
  • Available in large quantities to meet the demands of large projects.
  • Fast and reliable delivery service to job sites in Makkah.
  • Competitive prices that ensure the best value for quality.

Common Uses of Speez

  • Road Sub-bases: To create a strong and stable base for roads and pavements.
  • Foundation Backfilling: To fill gaps around foundations and provide extra support.
  • Heavy Leveling Work: In projects that require high load-bearing ground leveling.
  • Under Factory and Warehouse Floors: To ensure the stability of heavy industrial floors.
  • Infrastructure Works: In sanitation and water network projects as a supportive layer.
